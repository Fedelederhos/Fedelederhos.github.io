  • Latin American Innovation Rally 2021
    In a 28-hour time period, 8000 students and teachers from different universities gathered to develop a solution to various challenges. My team was composed of students from Mendoza, Cordoba, Buenos Aires and Chaco. Together, we chose the "Water for Dreams" challenge and developed a solution of which we are very satisfied with the results obtained. In its first stage, the solution would create a communal filtering system and storage in a water tank suitable for consumption, providing data to users through messages, and in a final instance a similar system implemented in each household. It would have 4 rainwater collection points that would have their own filters located in an outdoor park, these points would be three-layer physical filters, which would channel the filtered water to a tank, which would have an arduino, powered by solar panels, previously prepared to measure the volume of the tank, its chlorine concentration and electrical conductivity.
